Our goal is to inform Columbia Residents where gun free zones are located around the city. We believe that this knowledge will help keep our city safe and hope that this is useful to gun and non-owners alike.

About us:

We are three undergraduate USC students who are both concerned about the recent gun violence around the country (as well as in our city) and about protecting the residents of Columbia's gun rights.
